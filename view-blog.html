Egypt Travel Guide and Information | things to do in Egypt

  • Feb 15, 2015
  • Tour, Place, Trip, Country

Cairo, Egypt is a city with a rich history and culture. It's also a city that is constantly changing as it grows and develops. This can make it difficult to know where to go and what to see when you visit Cairo. Fortunately, our Cairo travel Guide is here to help!

Every year, millions of tourists come to Cairo's attractions, all for good reason. In addition to the Pyramids of Giza and the Great Sphinx, Cairo is home to a number of other notable Egyptian attractions, such as the Egyptian Museum. Visitors can also visit ancient churches like the Hanging Church and mosques like the al-Azhar Mosque, some of which are among the most respected in the world. Besides all the fascinating historical attractions, Cairo as a city tends to attract visitors. It has a unique appeal that you won't find anywhere else in the world. It is a busy and chaotic city, but it is also an enchanting place.

The Egyptian museum is considered the oldest archeological Museum in the Middle East, which includes the world's greatest antiquities from the Predynastic to the Greco-Roman era.

The Egyptian Museum in Tahrir, located in Tahrir square in downtown Cairo. The museum displayed about 120.000 artifacts from mummies, sarcophagi, ceramics, jewelry, and the most famous collection of King Tutankhamun's treasures like his golden mask which is the most glorious piece ever made by mankind. Enjoy one of our Egypt day tours which includes a tour to the Egyptian Museum to see the famous masterpieces of the ancient Egyptian civilization. The Egyptian Museum Tour takes about 2 hours with our guide who will give you a great introduction to the history of Egypt and help you to know more about Egypt during the different historical eras.
